How It Works

Step 1

We begin by applying a non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning solution that loosens deeply embedded dirt and grime from your floors and surfaces. This step is essential, as traditional cleaning methods often fail to fully remove built-up residue.

Step 2

Our patented cleaning system uses heated water at a precise, pre-selected pressure to thoroughly clean your surfaces. Simultaneously, the water is vacuum-extracted, leaving your floors clean, fresh, and quickly dry to the touch.
